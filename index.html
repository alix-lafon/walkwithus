Walk With Us

Take us with you for every back-home travel in Paris and we'll make it your most secure trip.

Worried about walking home alone at night after every party? Don't want to walk down a dangerous or scary street? Don't want to feel like you have to spend money on a taxi every night? We are here to help you!

Our aim is to make walking in Paris safe and to give women all the means to fight effectively against street harassment. Our short-term goal is to enable women to use the safest routes and return home without fear. Our long term goal is to stop street harassment and to raise awareness about this social problem.
